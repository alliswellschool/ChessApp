About Chess Activities

Chess Activities is an interactive educational platform designed to help chess enthusiasts of all levels improve their game through engaging puzzles, quizzes, and training activities.

Our mission is to make chess learning fun, accessible, and effective. Whether you're a beginner learning piece movements or an advanced player honing tactical skills, we provide tools to support your chess journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Chess Activities free to use?

Yes! All core features are completely free. We're supported by voluntary donations from users who appreciate the platform.

Do I need to create an account?

An account is required to access quizzes and track your progress. Puzzles and some activities are available without registration.

What skill levels do you support?

We cater to all levels from absolute beginners to advanced players. Our content includes varying difficulty levels to match your skill.

How can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Email us at bugs@chessactivities.com with details. We appreciate all feedback and constantly work to improve the platform.
